My First Webpage

Brian Zayas

During the pandemic I spent a lot of my time playing video games which lead to a lot of boredom and self-exploration. Eventually I found my passion for photography. When I initially got my camera I went through a phase of taking photos of everything I can. That helped me find my own style of what I preffered to shoot. One day I just took my camera to work, I currently work as a valet driver for the AC Marriot downtown and we park the cars on the 10th floor. It was a slow day so I went up to the 10th floor of the garage and took a 30 second timelapse photo of I-4 that is directly in front of the hotel. I really wasn't expecting much when I brought my camera that day but it ended up being my favorite photo I've ever taken.
